Modul 5: Simple Present & Present Continuous

Dalam modul ini, Anda akan belajar dua tenses dasar yang penting: Simple Present Tense untuk kebiasaan dan fakta, serta Present Continuous Tense untuk tindakan yang sedang berlangsung.

1. Simple Present Tense

The Simple Present Tense is used for:

Penggunaan (Usage):

  • Habits and Routines: Actions that happen regularly. (Kebiasaan dan Rutinitas: Tindakan yang terjadi secara teratur.)
  • General Truths and Facts: Things that are always true. (Kebenaran Umum dan Fakta: Hal-hal yang selalu benar.)
  • Schedules and Timetables: Fixed events like bus times, school classes. (Jadwal dan Jadwal Waktu: Acara yang tetap seperti jadwal bus, kelas sekolah.)

Struktur Kalimat (Sentence Structure):

  • Positive: Subject + Verb (s/es for he/she/it) + Object/Complement
  • Negative: Subject + do/does + not + Verb (base form) + Object/Complement
  • Question: Do/Does + Subject + Verb (base form) + Object/Complement?

Contoh Kalimat (Example Sentences):

Positive:

I eat breakfast every morning. (Saya makan sarapan setiap pagi.)

She reads a book every night. (Dia membaca buku setiap malam.)

The sun rises in the East. (Matahari terbit di Timur.)

Negative:

I do not drink coffee. (Saya tidak minum kopi.)

He does not work on weekends. (Dia tidak bekerja di akhir pekan.)

Question:

Do you live here? (Apakah kamu tinggal di sini?)

Does she like pizza? (Apakah dia suka pizza?)

2. Present Continuous Tense

The Present Continuous Tense is used for:

Penggunaan (Usage):

  • Actions Happening Now: What is happening at the moment of speaking. (Tindakan yang Sedang Berlangsung Sekarang: Apa yang terjadi saat berbicara.)
  • Temporary Actions: Actions that are happening around now, but not necessarily at this exact second. (Tindakan Sementara: Tindakan yang terjadi di sekitar waktu ini, tetapi tidak harus pada detik ini.)
  • Future Plans/Arrangements: For definite plans in the near future. (Rencana/Pengaturan Masa Depan: Untuk rencana pasti dalam waktu dekat.)

Struktur Kalimat (Sentence Structure):

  • Positive: Subject + am/is/are + Verb-ing + Object/Complement
  • Negative: Subject + am/is/are + not + Verb-ing + Object/Complement
  • Question: Am/Is/Are + Subject + Verb-ing + Object/Complement?

Contoh Kalimat (Example Sentences):

Positive:

I am studying English now. (Saya sedang belajar bahasa Inggris sekarang.)

She is watching TV. (Dia sedang menonton TV.)

They are playing soccer. (Mereka sedang bermain sepak bola.)

Negative:

I am not sleeping. (Saya tidak sedang tidur.)

He is not working today. (Dia tidak sedang bekerja hari ini.)

Question:

Are you listening? (Apakah kamu sedang mendengarkan?)

Is she cooking dinner? (Apakah dia sedang memasak makan malam?)

Latihan: Pilih Tense yang Tepat (Exercise: Choose the Correct Tense)

Pilih Simple Present Tense atau Present Continuous Tense yang paling tepat untuk melengkapi kalimat.

I often ___ (go) to the library.
