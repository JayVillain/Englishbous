Modul 4: Essential Vocabulary

Membangun kosakata adalah bagian inti dari belajar bahasa Inggris. Dalam modul ini, Anda akan mempelajari kata-kata penting dalam beberapa kategori dasar.

1. Keluarga (Family)

  • Mother Ibu My mother is very kind.
  • Father Ayah His father works in the city.
  • Brother Kakak/Adik laki-laki I have one brother.
  • Sister Kakak/Adik perempuan She has an older sister.
  • Son Anak laki-laki They have a son named David.
  • Daughter Anak perempuan My daughter loves to read.

2. Makanan & Minuman (Food & Drinks)

  • Apple Apel I like to eat an apple.
  • Water Air Please give me some water.
  • Bread Roti She eats bread for breakfast.
  • Coffee Kopi He drinks coffee every morning.
  • Rice Nasi We eat rice for lunch.
