Modul 16: Reading Comprehension

Selamat datang di modul terakhir! Reading Comprehension adalah kemampuan untuk memahami makna dari sebuah teks. Dalam modul ini, Anda akan membaca teks-teks pendek dan menjawab pertanyaan untuk melatih pemahaman Anda.

Teks Bacaan: "The Importance of Reading"

The Importance of Reading

Reading is one of the most important skills a person can develop. It opens up new worlds of knowledge, improves vocabulary, and enhances critical thinking. When we read, we expose ourselves to different ideas, cultures, and perspectives, which helps us grow as individuals.

Regular reading also strengthens our brain. It is a mental exercise that keeps our minds sharp and active. For students, reading comprehension is vital for academic success, as it allows them to understand textbooks, articles, and exam questions effectively.

Beyond academic benefits, reading can be a great source of entertainment and relaxation. Whether it's a thrilling novel, an informative non-fiction book, or a captivating news article, reading offers a quiet escape and a way to learn something new every day. So, pick up a book and start exploring!
